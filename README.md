# 🏥 The Clinic App

A comprehensive, user-friendly health assistant built with **Python** and **Streamlit**. This application aims to streamline basic health tracking and medical navigation by offering quick access to essential wellness tools.

## ✨ Key Features

* **BMI Calculator:** Calculate your Body Mass Index quickly to monitor your health trends.
* **Symptom Checker:** An interactive tool to help you assess symptoms and better understand potential health concerns.
* **Doctor Finder:** Easily locate medical professionals based on your specific location.
* **Emergency Assistance:** Quick-access information and resources for urgent situations.
